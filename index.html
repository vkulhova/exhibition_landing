EXHIBITION "PALAE"

An incredible exhibition that radically changes the idea of candle art

ABOUT THE ARTISTS

A year and a half ago, Aliona Nikolaieva and Tatiana Leonidova had their first thoughts about creating candles. Over the next few months, they tested all possible shapes and waxes and came to the conclusion that it was time to think about the concept. As of now, the craftswomen have developed a concept for candles that will be released in collections, each with its own special meaning.
